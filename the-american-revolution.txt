The American Revolution (1775-1783)

Description: 
The American Revolution was a war fought between Great Britain and thirteen American colonies, 
resulting in the establishment of the United States of America as an independent nation.

Additional Info: 
The American Revolution was sparked by grievances over British taxation, 
lack of representation, and the desire for self-governance. 
It led to the adoption of the Declaration of Independence and a prolonged military struggle. 
The revolution inspired other independence movements and played a pivotal role in the 
development of democratic principles and the spread of Enlightenment ideals globally.
