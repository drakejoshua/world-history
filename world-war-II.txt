World War II (1939-1945)

Description: 
World War II was a global conflict that involved most of the world's nations, 
fought between two major alliances: the Allies and the Axis powers. 
It arose as a result of territorial ambitions, ideological differences, 
and the failure of diplomatic efforts to prevent war.

Additional Info: 
World War II resulted in immense devastation, loss of life, 
and significant geopolitical changes. 
It witnessed the Holocaust, the use of atomic weapons, and the establishment of the United Nations. 
The war led to post-war rebuilding efforts, the decolonization of many nations, 
and the Cold War between the United States and the Soviet Union.
